About Us

Welcome to FaceGym! Our goal as a company is to help you be the best version of yourself. Traditional gyms can be very intimidating, especially when you’re new and unsure what you are doing. That’s why we want to make our gym as accessible and friendly as possible. We are a team of experienced fitness coaches that are all about YOU. Everything we do is designed around any athletic level. Whether you are an experienced athlete or it’s your first time at the gym, we make sure to accommodate you. Due to COVID-19 we make sure everything is sanitized before and after each client. Contact us and come join! At FaceGym we are all about the win!
